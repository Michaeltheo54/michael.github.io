The success story of Tom Cruise

Tom Cruise needs no introduction. He is undoubtedly one of the biggest movie stars in the world. He has ruled the box office for more than 30 years and has starred in numerous blockbuster films and has millions of fans all over the world.
His belief in himself, dedication to work and unstoppable ambition has led to him becoming one of the most outstanding and successful movie stars of our time.
Just like most successful people, his path to success was not a smooth one, and today we are going to explore his journey to success.
He was born on July 3, 1962, in Syracuse, New York. He was the third born and the only boy out of four children. His mother was a special education teacher while his father was an electrical engineer.
His family were faced with poverty and both his parents worked hard to provide for their children. Cruise could barely read in high school due to a disorder he was dealing with known as dyslexia, of which he was diagnosed with at the age of 7. It is a learning disorder which involves reading difficulty due to problems identifying speech sounds and learning how they relate to words and letters. He spent his childhood trying to hide this disorder from both his friends and classmates.
He determined that he would not let his condition stop him from achieving his dreams Actually, acting was never his top priority. He was already a star in the school's field hockey team, and even dreaming of becoming a sports star in the future. He was so dedicated that he spent most of his free time practising sports.
But tragedy came along the way, and sadly, he suffered from a knee injury, shattering his hopes of becoming a sports star. But, this incident served as a turning point in his life and made him choose a different career path, which was acting.
He then decided to join the school's stage production, and it turned out that acting satisfied his need for gaining attention. In addition, It helped him to cope with his dyslexia as memorizing scenes and lines improved his concentration and motivation. Having enjoyed his performance in the student's musical project of "Guys and Dolls," he finally decided that acting is his profession. He graduated from high school in 1980, and shortly after that, he moved to New York to follow his dreams. In order to support himself financially, he worked as a table-cleaner, a porter and a busboy. At the same he was working he also went to various auditions. He gave many auditions but got frequent rejections.
He tried many times and after so many trials he got a small role in 'Endless Love in 1981'. It was a small role but this was just the beginning. His acting was so impressive that he got a major role in a comedy film, 'Losin It' and got a distinct identity in the film which was released in 1983. This film was the turning point of his acting career.
He got another role in the movie "Risky Business" (1983), and it was this particular movie that made him a superstar, as a result of his impressive performance, excellent acting and attractive look.
His amazing performances and brilliant acting in the movies which followed, earned him his first nomination at Golden Globe Awards for Best Performance by an Actor. In 1986, he got a role as Lt. Pete 'Maverick' Mitchell in the worldwide hit, "Top Gun" which eventually became a box-office hit and the biggest hit of that year . He was recognized in the industry as a superstar and gained fame and success.
But that was not all, his 1988 film 'Rain Man' received the Academy Award for best film.
In 1989, he received his first 'Golden Globe Award' for the film 'Born on the Fourth July' In the year 2000, he got a Golden Award for 'Magnolia'. In 2006, he topped the list of the 100 most powerful celebrities in a list released by Forbes magazine.
After the massive success of "Top Gun", he went on to star in several successful Hollywood movies such as the Last Samurai, Collateral, Jerry Maguire, mission impossible and 'Mission Impossible' which was also a huge hit at the box office The success of these movies have made him become one of the greatest actors the movie industry has ever witnessed.
Tom Cruise's success story is truly an inspiring one. He overcame his learning disabilities, physical injury, childhood trauma and poverty to become the global superstar we know, and his work ethic, his belief in himself, dedication and persistence has made him one of the most recognized actors in the world
