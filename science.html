Artificial Intelligence: What it is, how it began, its present and future

Artificial intelligence (AI) is one of the buzzwords in tech right now, and with good reason. It is a method that allows computers, computer-controlled robots or software to think intelligently like the human mind. AI is done by studying the patterns of the human brain and analyzing cognitive processes. The results of these studies develop intelligent software and systems. Experts see artificial intelligence as a factor of production, with the potential to create new sources of growth and change the way work is done across industries.
